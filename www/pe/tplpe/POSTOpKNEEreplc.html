KNEE REPLACEMENT POST-OPERATIVE ORDERS
KNEE POST OPERATIVE FORM
To post-operative recovery room.
Anterior-Posterior (AP) & Lateral X-rays of operated knee. Be sure the patella and foot are facing up-right.
Regular diet as tolerated.
Keep IV per ordered by Anesthesiologist.
Keep epdural anesthesia per order by Anesthesiologist.
Demoral 50-75 mgs every 3-4 hrs prn.
Arcoxia 60, 90, 120 mgs every 6 hrs prn.
Aspirin 80 mgs, daily indefinitely.
Ferrous sulfate 300 mgs, three times a day for 3 months
Massive ICE packs around the hip area continuously for 48 hrs.
Measure and record drainage from Hemovac suction
Repeat IV Cefuroxime 1.5 gms in the OR before induction. Repeat 12 hrs. one doze only
Repeat IV Cefazoline 2 gms in 8 hrs one doze only
HgB and Hematocrit in 48 hrs
Keep legs apart with two pillows or abduction pillow
PHYSICAL THERAPHY 2X DAILY
Foot and Ankle pump 10 times every hour
Quadriceps and hamstring setting exercises 10 times every hour
Active hip flexion not more than 90°
Isomatic Abduction 10x every hour
Ambulation with crutches or walker, weight bearings as tolerated
 
Do not cross legs for at least 3 months
May dangle on bed with assistance the first post-operative day
When sitting, keep feet turn out. Do not internally rotate leg in flexion
Keep thigh high TEDS stockings when up or awake. Use TEDS stockings for the next 6 weeks in both legs.
Ambulate on the 2ND or 3RD with PT supervision or nurses assistance
